# Hong Kong Demographic Data Sources Research

## Primary Source: Census and Statistics Department

- **Website**: https://www.censtatd.gov.hk
- **Description**: Official source for census and demographic data
- **Access Method**: Website download or API (if available)
- **Available Years**: 2001, 2006, 2011, 2016

### Available Data:
- Population by TPU
- Age distribution
- Household income
- Education attainment
- Housing characteristics
- Employment statistics
